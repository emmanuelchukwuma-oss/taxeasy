========================================
PROJECT OVERVIEW
========================================

Design a mobile-first Nigerian tax filing and payment app called TaxEasy.
The app targets Nigerian SMEs, freelancers, salary earners with side income,
and informal earners. It must feel like a premium 100M Naira fintech product
— think Paystack, Moniepoint, and Stripe combined, but tuned for Nigerian
culture and trust signals.

The app is a single-page SPA rendered inside a mobile frame (390x844px,
iPhone 14 viewport). All screens except the dashboard have a persistent
sidebar on desktop (1440px wide), but mobile is the primary design target.

========================================
ADDITIONAL DESIGN NOTES
========================================

1. NIGERIAN CULTURAL SIGNALS
   Use N symbol everywhere (not NGN or USD).
   Show Nigerian flag emoji on phone number input.
   Reference FIRS (Federal Inland Revenue Service), NIBSS (Nigeria Inter-Bank
   Settlement System), and BVN/NIN only at payment — these build trust with Nigerian users.

2. TRUST SIGNALS THROUGHOUT
   Every screen involving ID data or payment should display a small trust strip:
   "NIBSS Secure · FIRS Compliant · 256-bit Encrypted"

3. GLASSMORPHISM ACCENTS
   The hero card on Welcome and the Payment Success screen use subtle glass overlays:
   rgba(255,255,255,0.72) backdrop with blur effect.

4. DARK MODE
   Not implemented — the app is light-mode only.
   Palette: forest green / white / mist.

5. ACCESSIBILITY
   All interactive elements need a minimum 44x44px tap target.
   Contrast ratio must be at least 4.5:1 for all body text.
   Focus rings must be visible on keyboard navigation.

6. RESPONSIVE BREAKPOINTS
   Mobile: 390px (primary design target)
   Tablet: 768px (same layout as mobile, wider cards)
